# Privacy Policy for Gandalf

**Last Updated:** January 9, 2026

## Introduction

Gandalf ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API and is **never sent to external servers**:

- **User Profile**: Name, age, profession, and personal goals (optional)
- **Browsing Statistics**: Aggregated data about pages visited, time spent, and activity patterns
- **Goals & Progress**: Your daily and weekly goals and completion status
- **Streak Data**: Your productivity streak information
- **Settings & Preferences**: Your extension settings and customization choices

### Optional Anonymous Analytics

With your **explicit consent**, we collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics to improve the extension:

**What We Collect (if you opt-in):**
- Feature usage (button clicks, modal opens)
- Time range selections
- Goal creation events (not content)
- Extension performance metrics

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- ❌ Your browsing history or URLs
- ❌ Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Goal content or insights text
- ❌ Any identifiable data

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data
Your locally stored data is used exclusively to:
- Generate personalized browsing insights
- Track your productivity goals and progress
- Provide customized recommendations
- Display your activity statistics

### Anonymous Analytics (Optional)
If you opt-in, anonymous analytics help us:
- Understand which features are most valuable
- Identify and fix bugs
- Improve user experience
- Prioritize new features

## Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage**: All personal data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store any of your data on external servers
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
- **Encryption**: Chrome's storage API uses encryption to protect your data

## Your Rights & Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Opt-Out of Analytics
- Navigate to Settings → Privacy
- Toggle "Anonymous Analytics" off
- Your choice is saved and respected immediately

### View Privacy Policy
- Access the full privacy policy anytime from Settings → Privacy
- Click "View Privacy Policy"

### Delete Your Data
- Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data
- You can also clear data manually through Chrome settings

### Export Your Data
- All data is stored in Chrome's local storage
- You can access it through Chrome DevTools if needed

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics (Optional)
If you opt-in to analytics, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. Google Analytics may use cookies. Learn more: [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Groq API
We use Groq's API to generate browsing insights. Only aggregated, anonymous browsing statistics are sent to Groq for analysis. No personal information, browsing history, or URLs are shared. Learn more: [Groq Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/)

## Children's Privacy

Gandalf is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The extension requires users to be at least 13 years old during onboarding.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the extension (for significant changes)

## GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):
- **Right to Access**: You can access all your data through the extension
- **Right to Deletion**: Uninstall the extension to delete all data
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Disable analytics anytime in Settings
- **Data Portability**: Your data is stored locally and accessible to you
- **Consent**: We obtain explicit consent before collecting any analytics data

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents:
- We do not sell your personal information
- You have the right to opt-out of data collection (analytics toggle)
- You have the right to delete your data (uninstall extension)
- You have the right to know what data we collect (outlined above)
